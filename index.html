A World Without Barriers

A World Without Barriers is a non-profit project that was developed by myself and my friend Nicholas Nwanochie. This Application is intended to helping a non-profit raise funds by means of selling or donations. With a Database that contains separate tables for Users (people who wish to buy or donate), Items (Items for sale), Donations, and Admin. It logs items that are posted for sale or donations and it gives Admins the ability to delete the items once they are no longer available. By using our knowledge of Express Session and Authentication the website is fully secure to use for anyone who wishes to buy an item. It assures buyers that their credit card information is secure.
